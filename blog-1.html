MADOW

Grunge fashion is defined by sturdy, classic thrift shop clothes that is frequently worn loosely and androgynously to minimize the silhouette. Therefore, for this outfit, the top is black with fringe detailing, and the bottom is a pair of skintight distressed jeans in the same color. To complete the style, add some hefty jewelry, brown lipstick, and dark eye makeup. For footwear, use chunky white shoes.
