ORIEN

Fusion fashion is the blending of two or more culturally distinct fashions into one. Any culture may be incorporated into the mix; however it seems recently that western and Indian styles have gotten greater attention. Traditional days or functions in colleges, workplaces, or the neighborhood scream out the requirement for an Indo-Western appearance. So, an emerald green pair of corduroy trousers and a red sleeveless short kurti with print are worn together, and red sandals that match the kurti are added along with some oxidized jewellery to complete the outfit. This adds even more simplicity to the basic, traditional look.
