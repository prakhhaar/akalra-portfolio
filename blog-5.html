NINTENDO

By layering many outfits on top of one another, one may create the look of layered clothes. In order to wear the essentials in a different way, a pair of cycling shorts are combined with a basic black turtleneck full-sleeved top and on top of it is an oversized black t-shirt with digital print on the back and front both and to finish the look some silver jewellery, a black backpack and knee-length black socks and platform boots with no laces fastening are then added to the ensemble to make it both practical and attractive.
