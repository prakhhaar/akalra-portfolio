SAYEE

The saree may be one of the oldest pieces of clothing, and as time has gone on, it has been worn in a variety of ways. In this look, a black saree with a blue border is worn over a simple black turtleneck top and black gym pants with net detailing, if tights are in style, tights as a piece of clothing are here to stay. A pair of black streetwear flops pull the whole outfit together, while the makeup is quite simple with blue eyeliner and darker lipstick, earnings are on the heavier side for the look.
