Hello, I'm Aishwarya.

Pursuing Fashion Designing,

at Woxsen University.

I am a Fashion Designer with experience in all designing aspects and I am zealous about pattern making, also have keen interest in make-up and hair-style. My designs and inspirations have mostly have had a nature's touch to them. I try to siphon the beauty of enigmas of the mystical phenomenon this world must bestow.

I try to have a rich and acceptable nature which will ultimately help me in becoming a better working partner and I believe in creating a positive environment for the team and everyone present around me. The work ethic and dedication I have towards my work is something that helps me to be different than the crowd. I aspire to be the best in what I do and I am very committed to my work.
